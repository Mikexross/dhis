About us

Deluxe Home Inspection Services was founded on the principals of service. Serving our clients as a team member, assisting the sales process by informing the customer that inspection issues are resolvable. Serving our customers with a thorough home inspection designed to inform them of their future home's health. Making them an informed, confident, and comfortable buyer. We take every effort to make this a positive experience for all parties.

And finally, we do our part to serve the community. Deluxe Home Inspection Services has aligned with Elijah's Heart, a local non-profit organization that supports at-risk children by providing food and education. When we give our children better health and education, we give them hope for a better future.
